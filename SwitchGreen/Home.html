Latest Findings

October 2025

India accelerates toward 500 GW renewable capacity

India's renewable energy capacity reached 200.30 GW, marking 40% progress toward the 2030 target. Solar PV continues to dominate with 129.92 GW, driven by competitive tariffs and government schemes like PM-KUSUM.

State Analysis

Top 5 states contribute 75% of total capacity

Rajasthan leads with 40.78 GW, followed by Gujarat (38.88 GW), Maharashtra (25.24 GW), Tamil Nadu (24.58 GW), and Karnataka (21.99 GW). These states account for three-quarters of India's renewable power.
