üå± About Switch-Green

Switch-Green is India's comprehensive renewable energy data portal, created as a Computer Engineering microproject to track and visualize the nation's progress toward the ambitious 500 GW renewable energy target by 2030.

Project Overview

Our platform aggregates official data from the Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE) and presents it through interactive dashboards, detailed state-wise comparisons, and educational resources. We aim to make India's renewable energy data accessible, understandable, and actionable for citizens, policymakers, students, and researchers.

Key Features

  • ‚úÖ Real-time Data: Official MNRE statistics updated monthly
  • ‚úÖ Interactive Dashboards: Visual charts and analytics
  • ‚úÖ State Comparison: Detailed breakdown of all 37 states
  • ‚úÖ AQI Impact Analysis: Air quality improvements from renewable adoption
  • ‚úÖ Educational Content: Learn about green energy and how to contribute
  • ‚úÖ Downloadable Reports: Export data in JSON, CSV, and Excel formats

Our Mission

To empower every Indian with accurate, accessible renewable energy data and inspire action toward a sustainable, clean energy future. We believe that informed citizens drive meaningful change.
